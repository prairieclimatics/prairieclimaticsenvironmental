About Us

Prairie Climatics Environmental is a trusted environmental sampling business serving Saskatchewan farmers and agricultural operations. With expertise in soil and water analysis, we help farmers make data-driven decisions to improve yields while ensuring environmental safety and compliance.

Our commitment is to provide accurate, timely results that help protect your land, water, and livestock.
